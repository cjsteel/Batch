PLP Migration notes.txt

When reinstalling a users PLP you may need to manually copy her/is data 
to our new installation location.

After your new installation is completed go to the old installation
point (generally C:\PartListPlus and copy the following directories:

configurations
and
reports

(See Eddy if clarifiation is required)

The will be pasted over the corresponding folders in the new 
installation location at 

C:\Documents and Settings\%USERNAME%\PartListLpus
